FREE SHIPPING, FREE RETURNS

expand_more

SHIPPING
Free delivery within 1-7 business days. Most orders deliver within 1-3 business days. Items transferred from Europe can take up to 7 business days. Pre-order deliveries vary. Orders placed by 4pm ET Monday to Friday are processed that day, except for federal holidays. All other orders are processed the following business day. Some orders may require additional validation that can take 24-36 hours, which may delay delivery. Adult signature is required on all shipments.

RETURNS
Free returns and exchanges within 30 days from the date of delivery. Exchanges on item's size only by contacting customer service or visiting a store. In-store return and exchange policies differ. Please contact your local Balenciaga store for details.
